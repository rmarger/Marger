---
title: Florida Supreme Court clarifies parental relocation standard
type: caselaw
date: March 2026
blurb: The court reinforced that a parent seeking to relocate must demonstrate the move is in the child's best interest, not merely the parent's convenience. This decision strengthens protections for the non-relocating parent.
---

The Florida Supreme Court issued a significant ruling clarifying the burden of proof in parental relocation cases under F.S. §61.13001. The decision makes clear that the relocating parent bears the burden of demonstrating the move serves the child's best interest across all statutory factors — a higher bar than some lower courts had applied.
