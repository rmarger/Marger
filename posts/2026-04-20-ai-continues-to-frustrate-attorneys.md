Rodriguez v. Rodriguez, 6D25-3086, April 10, 2026 - AI generated numerous cases, which counsel cited in a Petition. The Court issued an Order to Show Cause as to why the attorney should not be sanctioned and referred to the Florida bar for discipline. 

Take Away: Verify everything. Your reputation and your license is at stake. AI isn't to the point of being trustwothy... yet.
